HOSTEL RULES

Hostels are vibrant spaces that bring together students from diverse backgrounds and experiences. To ensure a comfortable and safe living environment for everyone, it's important to establish clear rules and regulations that promote respect, responsibility, and community spirit. These guidelines are not simply restrictions; they are the foundation for a positive and enriching hostel experience. By adhering to them, we can create a space where everyone feels welcome, valued, and supported. Some of the rules are:

no stealing

Adeleke University Hostel Management strongly opposes theft. We're committed to a safe, respectful community. Keep belongings secure; let's ensure a positive living experience for everyone.

no fighting

At Adeleke University Hostel Management, we prioritize a peaceful community. Avoid conflicts and contribute to a positive environment. Let's make the hostel a safe and harmonious space for all.!

no bullying

Adeleke University Hostel Management stands against bullying. Foster a culture of kindness and respect. Let's create a supportive community where everyone feels valued and safe.

no protesting

Adeleke University Hostel Management upholds a peaceful living environment. Please refrain from protesting within the hostel premises. Let's maintain a harmonious atmosphere for everyone.
